**Module:** Strategic Management & Leadership
**Academic Year:** 2024-2025

## Key Topics Covered

### Week 1: Introduction to Strategy
- What is strategy?
- Course overview and expectations
- Chapters 1, 11.4, and 11.5

### Week 2: External Analysis
- Industry and market analysis
- Competitive environment assessment
- Porter's Five Forces
- Chapters 2 and 3

### Week 3: Internal Analysis
- Resource-based view
- Core competencies and capabilities
- SWOT Analysis framework
- Chapters 4 and 5

### Week 4: Strategy Formulation I
- Strategic option development
- Feasibility checks
- TOWS Matrix (Threats-Opportunities-Weaknesses-Strengths)
- Chapters 6, 7, and 8

### Week 5: Strategy Formulation II
- Strategic choices and selection
- Business-level and corporate-level strategies
- Competitive strategies (cost leadership, differentiation, focus)
- Chapters 5, 7, and 8

### Week 6: Strategic Leadership
- Role of leadership in strategy execution
- Strategic leadership principles
- Leadership styles and strategic decision-making
- Chapters 10 and 11

### Week 7: Organizational Systems & Change
- Organizational structure and strategy alignment
- Change management
- Implementation challenges
- Chapter 9
